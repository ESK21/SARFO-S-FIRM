# AC Light Dimmer Circuit

## Overview
A mains-powered light dimmer circuit designed for AC load control.

## Key Features
- FL5150MX driver IC
- MOSFET switching (IRF840)
- Fuse and diode protection
- Adjustable dimming control

## Design Considerations
- High voltage safety
- Isolation and protection
- Heat and switching behavior

## Files
- Schematic
- PCB Layout
- 3D Design
 
⚠️ Note: This design involves high voltage (mains). Proper isolation, enclosure, and safety precautions are required in real-world implementation.
